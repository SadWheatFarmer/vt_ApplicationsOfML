List of datasets and important information

1. QCOM_HistoricalData_5yr.csv
Desc:       5 years of DAILY data about the  Qualcomm company stock price.
Features:   Date,       Close/Last, Volume, Open,   High,   Low
Types:      mm/dd/yyyy  float       int     float   float   float
Freq:       Daily
URL:        https://www.nasdaq.com/market-activity/stocks/qcom/historical
Impact:     REQUIRED



2. marketYield_2YrUsTreasurySecurity_5yr.csv
    marketYield_10YrUsTreasurySecurity_5yr.csv
Desc:       5 years of daily PERCENTAGE change in the 2 & 10 year maturity US
             Security. These values reflect the US Treasury Department's overall
             sentiment of the economy.
             Good Economy when: 10Yr% < 2Yr%
Freq:       Daily
URL:        https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/DGS10
Impact:     Is related to overall economic forecast



3.  globalPriceOfBrentCrudeOil_5y
Desc:       Global price of crude oil (all types) sold around the world.
Freq:       Monthly
URL:        https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/POILBREUSDM
Impact:     THIS DATA IS INCOMPLETE. It only goes up too 11/01/2021. I think it
             still can be used but we may need to forecast to 2022.



4.  copper_globalPricePerMetricTon_5yr
Freq:   Monthly
URL:    https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/PCOPPUSDM

5.  aluminum_globalPricePerMetricTon_5yr
Freq:   Monthly
URL:    https://fred.stlouisfed.org/series/PALUMUSDM

6.  QCOM-SimFin-data.csv (company fundamentals)
Freq:   Quarterly
